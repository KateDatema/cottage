The Beach and Lake

The cottage has a private, south facing, sandy beach and dock. We provide chairs that are perfect for laying out in the sun and watching the kids play. The lake is very clean and is great for swimming and boating. A canoe and rowboat are available with your rental, kayaks are available on request for an extra fee. Feel free to bring your boat (launch at the public launch) and enjoy tubing, skking, and drives around the lake.   Enjoy evenings around the fire pit roasting marshmallows and hotdogs right on the beach (Wood available for purchase at various locations around the lake). 

Please note wake hours are 11am - 7:30pm

Kitchen

The kitchen is has all the regular common kitchen utensils. The cottages has an electric stove/oven and Bosch dishwasher. There are two full size refrigerators (one in the kitchen and one downstairs)   There is also an outside gas grill and small charcoal grill available.

For meals there is two outdoor picnic tables on cement patio and indoor dining tables (which seats 16).

Interent, Booster + TV

Internet is avaible, most of the time it works well, however we don’t recieve the best internet in this areas.

A Verizon wireless booster is run off of the internet. If you have Verizon this will give your phone more bars, better signal, and faster internet.

Roku TV is availble, it receives a few channels. streaming can be a hit or miss.
